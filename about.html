Hotel Shahi Haveli - Fine dining hotel and restaurant

Have an enjoyable dining experience at our multi-cuisine Hotel & restaurant 'Hotel Shahi Haveli' in a quiet hospitable environment, where good food is always served with a smile. A placid and soothing environment is set up for you to enjoy your meal. Indulge in a dining experience that will stimulate all your senses with the mouth-watering dishes tantalizing your taste buds. Our hotel is famous in the fardkot city for its Indian,Italian,South Indian,Subway and Chinese food .Our punjabi food is the most popular amongt our guests. Come, join us for fine dining at our hotel. We at Hotel Shahi Haveli Kotkapura road, Faridkot offer you a memorable dining experience with exceptionally delicious Punjabi dishes.
